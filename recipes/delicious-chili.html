Delicious Chili Recipe

Description

A delicious chili recipe taught to me by Esther. Great stuff. Amazing. Tasty. Delicious. Savory. Very healthy. Lots of protein. Did I mention delicious? Nice beans, nice ground turkey, nice savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Turkey
  • 1 can (15-16oz) pinto beans
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomato
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Sour cream
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tortilla
  • McCormick Mild Chili Seasoning Mix

Instructions

  1. Dice some onions and fry in low mid heat until juices come out
  2. Put in 1lb turkey meat and cook until done
  3. Put in some beans
  4. Put in some water and chili packet sauce (follow instructions on package)
  5. Simmer on low heat until less liquid
  6. Mix with chopped lettuce tomato salsa sour cream
  7. Rip some warm tortilla into the mix
  8. Enjoy delicious meal
